📖 5 Verbal Ability Questions with Answers

Q1. Choose the correct sentence:
A) She don’t like coffee.
B) She doesn’t like coffee.
Answer: B) She doesn’t like coffee.

Q2. Synonym of “Brief”
Answer: Short

Q3. Fill in the blank:
He insisted ____ going there.
Answer: On

Q4. Identify the error:
Each of the players have a jersey.
Answer: “have” → should be “has”

Correct sentence: Each of the players has a jersey.

Q5. Antonym of “Expand”
Answer: Contract
